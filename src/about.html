About Us
We’re a group of creative thinkers who have innovated a prototype to change the transportation world.

The Automated Cargo Truck is a revolutionary advancement in logistics, designed to transport cargo from ship to warehouse seamlessly. Equipped with a sophisticated RFID scanner, it reads tags placed under each container, which detail the container’s destination. This ensures precise delivery without manual intervention.
With its autonomous navigation system, the truck follows optimized routes to the designated warehouse, enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs. By minimizing the need for manual labor, it also decreases the risk of human error. The system is capable of handling large volumes of cargo, making it ideal for high-traffic ports and logistics hubs.
Incorporating advanced RFID technology and autonomous driving, the Automated Cargo Truck significantly boosts operational efficiency and reliability. This innovation meets the demands of modern supply chains, ensuring that cargo is delivered accurately and swiftly, thereby supporting businesses in maintaining a competitive edge in a fast-paced environment.
